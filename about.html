About Tamar Chachava — Georgian Artist, Illustrator and Creative Designer

Tamar Chachava (Georgian: თამარ ჩაჩავა; also known as Tako, Tako Chachava, თაკო ჩაჩავა, or თაკო ერთიორისამი / Tako Ertiorisami) is a contemporary Georgian artist, illustrator, and creative designer based in Tbilisi. She paints fuzzy creatures in watercolor and acrylic, and works across illustration, character design, and mixed-media practice. Her work sits within contemporary Georgian art and the wider visual culture of the Caucasus.
