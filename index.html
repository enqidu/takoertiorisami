Tamar Chachava · Tako Chachava · თამარ ჩაჩავა · თაკო ჩაჩავა — Georgian Artist, Illustrator and Creative Designer

Tamar Chachava (Georgian: თამარ ჩაჩავა; also known as Tako Chachava, თაკო ჩაჩავა, Tako, თაკო, or თაკო ერთიორისამი / Tako Ertiorisami) is a contemporary Georgian artist, illustrator and creative designer based in Tbilisi, Georgia. Her practice spans watercolor, acrylic painting, drawing, and mixed media. She is best known for her hand-painted fuzzy creatures, warm color palettes, and a playful, painterly visual voice rooted in Georgian contemporary art.

Name variants: Tamar Chachava, Tako Chachava, Tako, თამარ ჩაჩავა, თაკო ჩაჩავა, თაკო, ჩაჩავა თამარ, ჩაჩავა თაკო, Chachava Tamar, Chachava Tako, თაკო ერთიორისამი, Tako Ertiorisami.

This portfolio collects her paintings, illustrations, and creative design work.
